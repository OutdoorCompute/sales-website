Case studies:

  • Dark Green LLC:
    • use case: Working towards a green society is a key pillar of Dark Green LLC. They do not only offer consultancy on energy green solutions but have also invested heavily in their own eco-friendly company. This results in an excess energy production of their solar panels. The excess electric power is consumed by the Cloud Unit, instead of delivering the excess electric power back to the energy grid at a low price. The business does not only uses their excess electric power more effective but can also use the waste heat from the Cloud Unit to heat their building, reducing heating expenses.
    • How can the Cloud Unit add value?:
      • Use excess green energy more effective, OutdoorCompute has found an IT company that is willing to run their batch jobs in the Cloud Units that are located at Dark Green LLC, especially between 11:00 and 18:00. Dark Green LLC has shutdown their Central heating system because Cloud Unit provides enough heat for the whole office.
  • Jan Jansen:
    • use case: Jan Jansen has build a beautiful new private building. The building is connected to the electricity grid but no other sources of energy are available (gas connection or district heating). He is forced to use an electric boiler for heating his building. This is possible because he has a well isolated building and solar panels that generate electricity.
    • How can the Cloud Unit add value?:
      • Eliminate energy bill (heating), Jan Jansen invests in a Cloud Unit. The electric energy is now used for an useful purpose (cloud) and the "free waste heat" can be used for heating the private building.
      • No maintenance, OutdoorCompute handles everything, he does not have to worry about maintaining the Cloud Unit.
      • Passive income, he will not only eliminate his electric bill due heating but also receives interest on its investment.
