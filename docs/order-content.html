Do you need heat?

You can benefit by using the heat that is produced by the Micro Data Center. Just like solar panels, the Micro Data Center can be a valuable tool to become self-sufficient and green.

We can swiftly deploy a Micro Data Center but we will need some information to ensure a smooth integration. We will help you with answering the following questions:

  1. What is your heat requirement (kWh) and which outlet temperature is desired?
  2. Which Micro Data Center configuration is best suited for your situation (cooling tower, buffer tank).
  3. Is your current network connection sufficient?
  4. And discuss the possible location and integration with existing heating systems

Case studies:

  • Excess electric powerA business has a building that produces excess green energy by their solar installation. The excess electric power is consumed by the Micro Data Center, instead of delivering the excess electric power back to the energy grid at a low price. The business does not only uses their excess electric power more effective but can also use the waste heat from the Micro Data Center to heat their building, reducing heating expenses.
  • Electric boilerA individual is using electricity to produce heat (air or water). He/she made the smart decision to use the waste heat of the Micro Data Center for heating. The electricity is now "free" because it is first use for a useful purpose (computing).
