Green

Energy serves a dual purpose, first for computing and then for heating, maximizing its efficiency.

uDC Types

Heat reuse
Ideal for uDC owners who require continuous heat year-round. For example swimming pool or aquatic ponds.
Heat storage tanks
Do you frequently need additional heat during the day? The buffer tank can store up to ~34kWh of energy, ready to be used when extra heat is needed. Ideal for offices,residential housing, industrial processes or laundry facilities. The μDC can be utilized for heating both tap water and providing radiant floor heating.
Cooling towers
Is your primary concern focused on compute power and low latency? By incorporating the cooling towers, we can efficiently dissipate heat that isn't reused. We offer standard-configured μDCs (optimized for compute, storage, and AI), but we also provide the flexibility to configure the μDC to meet your unique use-case requirements.
