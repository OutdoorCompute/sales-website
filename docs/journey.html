2024 June 7 - Cloud Unit Impression

Before deploying our Cloud Unit to the customer location, we had the valuable opportunity to introduce it to some of our strategic partners and investors. At our company, we are passionate about hard work and committed to building a sustainable future for data centers. Realizing this vision would be impossible—and far less enjoyable—without collaborating with passionate and forward-thinking people.

Our network of Cloud Units allows companies worldwide to effortlessly enhance their Azure cloud, making it more cost-effective, truly sustainable, and green. At the installation site, it allows solar panels or wind turbines owners the chance to earn extra income by selling their excess localized green electricity to the network.

We extend our sincere thanks to everyone who attended the meeting on such short notice (within just 7 days). For those who couldn’t make it or were not invited, don’t worry—we will be hosting another session at the Cloud Unit’s operational site soon.

2024 April 04 - Internships

Today, we had the opportunity to deliver a presentation at the University of Amsterdam (UvA). We gained invaluable insights into the needs and interests of the students. During the presentation, Jacques introduced internship profiles:

1. Network Intrusion Detection: Join our team for an internship dedicated to pioneering AI-based intrusion detection systems (IDS) for our Cloud Unit gateway. You will contribute to cutting-edge research and implementation projects, exploring the integration of AI to enhance cloud security. This role is ideal for computer science or cybersecurity enthusiasts, offering hands-on experience in developing solutions to secure cloud infrastructures against evolving threats.

2. Smartgrid Integration: Research and develop a solution to synchronize compute power consumption with energy supply. This opportunity is perfect for individuals passionate about tackling energy challenges through technology. Join our project to make a tangible impact on sustainable computing practices.

3. Customized internships: We also offer customized internships tailored to suit individual needs. Our focus is on research, proof of concept, and implementation. Depending on the skill set and internship requirements, students can engage in one or all of these three phases.

If any of the following keywords spark your interest, please feel free to reach out to us to learn more about our internship program:

  • SmartGrid
  • Cybersecurity
  • Python
  • Scripting
  • Data Centers
  • Fiber Network
  • Linux, FreeBSD, OpenBSD
  • Performance Optimization
  • Enthusiastic Team and Startup Environment

Join our team and contribute to making a sustainable impact on the IT industry!

2024 January 09 - ACE Interview with Justus

Today we had an interview with Justus and his team. They are making a portrait of all the ACE incubator program candidates. Bellow you can find the questions and answers. The video will follow soon.

(Justus): What problem is your startup solving?
(Nick): Data Centers has become essential but use a lot of energy. The number of data centers will continue to grow in the future. How can we support the growth in an environmental and sustainable matter?

(Justus): Who are you and what drives you to solve this problem?
(Nick): I am Nick, I feel privileged to work on a problem that involves my entrepreneurial and technical interests. Especially because the solution has a positive environmental en social impact.

(Justus): What sets your startup apart from others in your industry?
(Nick): We believe that most startups and companies are valuable. Together we can bring new innovations and provide services or products that improve the quality of life. I admire our culture, we encourage to fail-fast, iterate, test, learn, share information and collaborate.

(Justus): What impact are you creating?
(Jacques): We are making compute power more affordable, sustainable and enabling ordinary people to have a financial benefit.

(Justus): What was your proudest achievement during the program?
(Nick): We are proud of all the people that have directly or indirectly helped us. Without them this would not be possible.

(Justus): What are your hopes & dreams for your startup in the (near) future?
(Jacques): Together we want to build thousands of Cloud Units and place them where the greatest impact can be made. As a society we are on our way but we can use energy much more effective think of solar panels, energy and heat storage, energy saving and the smart grid.

(Justus): Three words what you and/or your startup describes.
(Nick): Innovative, passionate, scalable
