The Journey

2024 February 09 - Inception Programs

We are excited to announce that we have been accepted into the NVIDIA Inception Program, joining a select group of startups poised to accelerate their adoption of cutting-edge technologies in rendering and AI. This program offers invaluable resources such as technical support, product discounts, and access to investors and communities.

In addition to our participation in the NVIDIA Inception Program, we are also proud members of the AWS and Azure programs. AWS has played a crucial role in supporting our journey by facilitating testing and development of our virtual micro data centers. Similarly, Azure has been instrumental in validating the seamless integration of our solutions with Azure Functions (FaaS).

We are immensely grateful for the support provided by these programs and remain committed to continued collaboration wherever possible. Together, we are driving towards a more sustainable future for computing!

2024 January 25 - Graduation ACE incubator

Today marks a significant milestone for us as we proudly announce our graduation from the ACE incubator. The journey through this accelerator program has been nothing short of transformative. Each week, we were immersed in invaluable knowledge sessions covering a diverse range of topics including law, intellectual property, marketing, sales, financing, IT certifications, and business model innovation, ensuring that every facet of OutdoorCompute was meticulously addressed.

Throughout this journey, the guidance and support of our mentors and coaches have been instrumental. Additionally, the ACE team has been an indispensable resource, providing us with everything we could have possibly needed. We were afforded numerous opportunities to test, refine, and receive feedback on our final presentation, which we delivered with confidence today.

We are immensely grateful for the enriching experience and look forward to applying the lessons learned as we continue to grow and thrive in the ever-evolving landscape of technology entrepreneurship.

2024 January 09 - ACE Interview with Justus

Today we had an interview with Justus and his team. They are making a portrait of all the ACE incubator program candidates. Bellow you can find the questions and answers. The video will follow soon.

(Justus): What problem is your startup solving?
(Nick): Data Centers has become essential but use a lot of energy. The number of data centers will continue to grow in the future. How can we support the growth in an environmental and sustainable matter?

(Justus): Who are you and what drives you to solve this problem?
(Nick): I am Nick, I feel privileged to work on a problem that involves my entrepreneurial and technical interests. Especially because the solution has a positive environmental en social impact.

(Justus): What sets your startup apart from others in your industry?
(Nick): We believe that most startups and companies are valuable. Together we can bring new innovations and provide services or products that improve the quality of life. I admire our culture, we encourage to fail-fast, iterate, test, learn, share information and collaborate.

(Justus): What impact are you creating?
(Jacques): We are making compute power more affordable, sustainable and enabling ordinary people to have a financial benefit.

(Justus): What was your proudest achievement during the program?
(Nick): We are proud of all the people that have directly or indirectly helped us. Without them this would not be possible.

(Justus): What are your hopes & dreams for your startup in the (near) future?
(Jacques): Together we want to build thousands of Micro Data Centers and place them where the greatest impact can be made. As a society we are on our way but we can use energy much more effective think of solar panels, energy and heat storage, energy saving and the smart grid.

(Justus): Three words what you and/or your startup describes.
(Nick): Innovative, passionate, scalable

2023 December 13 - ACE Pitch and Beers

ACE facilitated a great "Pitch and Beers" event. We brought our stress-test micro data center version to give the attendees an idea of our product. 2023 October 11 - Green Data Center Conference

We have visited a lot of events related to Big Data & AI, sustainable energy and data centers in the months October and November. We spoke to hundreds of companies and specialist in these fields. We want to thank everyone that gave feedback on our solution or insight in the problems that they solve. It was an enlightening experience.

What are some of the goals we accomplished?

  • New sustainable collaborations, suppliers and customers.
  • Further identified the needs of businesses that rely on compute power, AI and storage.
  • More insight into the data center heat waste reuse issue from different stakeholder perspectives: data centers, government, technology and energy companies.
https://www.dutchdatacenters.nl/agenda/green-data-center-conference/

2023 September 15 - EuroBSDCon 2023

We thoroughly relished our experience at EuroBSDCon 2023. The event proved to be not only excellent in terms of content and organization but also unfolded in an incredibly delightful location steeped in rich history. It was a perfect blend of informative sessions and a captivating venue that added an extra layer of enjoyment to the overall experience.

We believe that we can build a great startup if we work together with specialists who are passionate about the technology stack. And want to use their skills to build a more sustainable world and have a positive social impact on society. It would even be better if we could re-contribute to opensource technologies that we love and use every day.

One of our objectives was to immerse ourselves in the community atmosphere and gain a genuine understanding of its dynamics. Our aim was to explore the future collaborations and how we might work together.

https://2023.eurobsdcon.org/
