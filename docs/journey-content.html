The Journey

2023 December 13 - ACE Pitch and Beers

ACE facilitated a great "Pitch and Beers" event. We brought our stress-test micro data center version to give the attendees an idea of our product. We gave a 3 minute pitch to a jury and audience consisting of mentors, VCs and other stakeholders. We receive excellent questions from the jury and audience. It gave us the opportunity to evaluate the response on our business strategy and product.

2023 October 24 - Accepted into the ACE-incubator program!

We are embarking on a transformative journey through a rigorous 10-week program designed to fortify every facet of our startup. Following the intensive program, ACE will continue to offer unwavering support, providing ongoing guidance as needed to steer us toward sustained success.

https://ace-incubator.nl/

2023 October 11 - Green Data Center Conference

We have visited a lot of events related to Big Data & AI, sustainable energy and data centers in the months October and November. We spoke to hundreds of companies and specialist in these fields. We want to thank everyone that gave feedback on our solution or insight in the problems that they solve. It was an enlightening experience.

What are some of the goals we accomplished?

  • New sustainable collaborations, suppliers and customers.
  • Further identified the needs of businesses that rely on compute power, AI and storage.
  • More insight into the data center heat waste reuse issue from different stakeholder perspectives: data centers, government, technology and energy companies.
https://www.dutchdatacenters.nl/agenda/green-data-center-conference/

2023 September 15 - EuroBSDCon 2023

We thoroughly relished our experience at EuroBSDCon 2023. The event proved to be not only excellent in terms of content and organization but also unfolded in an incredibly delightful location steeped in rich history. It was a perfect blend of informative sessions and a captivating venue that added an extra layer of enjoyment to the overall experience.

We believe that we can build a great startup if we work together with specialists who are passionate about the technology stack. And want to use their skills to build a more sustainable world and have a positive social impact on society. It would even be better if we could re-contribute to opensource technologies that we love and use every day.

One of our objectives was to immerse ourselves in the community atmosphere and gain a genuine understanding of its dynamics. Our aim was to explore the future collaborations and how we might work together.

https://2023.eurobsdcon.org/

2023 April 21 - Co-Lab YES!DELFT

The Co-Labs provided us with invaluable opportunities to connect with fellow founders, fostering a vibrant network within our entrepreneurial community. These sessions were not only enriching but also included informative discussions on effective strategies for identifying and attracting new co-founders, emphasizing key characteristics essential for successful partnerships.

Yes!Funded played a pivotal role by enlightening us about diverse funding options and subsidies available for our startup. Through their extensive network, they facilitated crucial connections with key contacts, including those from RVO and other subsidy providers. We express our sincere gratitude for the invaluable support and insights received.
